Tom Joy is a current DPhil student at the University of Oxford and is a member of the [Torr Vision Group](http://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~tvg/). His current work is focussed on developing algorithms that efficiently learn how to perform new tasks whilst still retaining task-specific knowledge from previous tasks. This research direction is known as continual learning, or incremental learning, where the primary objective is to prevent catastrophic forgetting in the algorithm. Achieving such methods is a key component for deploying 'AI' systems in the wild, where it is essential that they are able to continually improve and learn to adapt.
